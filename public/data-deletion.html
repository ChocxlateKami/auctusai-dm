Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

To request deletion of your data from AuctusAI DM:

  1. Send an email to jasper.auctusai@gmail.com
  2. Include "Data Deletion Request" in the subject line
  3. Provide your Instagram username or account email
  4. We will process your request within 30 days

What Gets Deleted

We will delete:

  • Your Instagram account connection
  • All stored message data
  • Automation settings and configurations
  • Analytics and usage data

Processing Time

Data deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receipt.
